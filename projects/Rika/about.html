About Me

Rika Pinto is a 14 year old, Singer-Songwriter and actress.

Rika started singing at 5 years old, but ever since she was little she loved music, and taking pictures.

At only 12 Rika performed at the Young Artist Awards.

Rika has acted as delilah in Escape the night season 2, Oshani in SociCircle, Young Vidya in Khol and several commercials.

She writes and composes her own music & has also won numerous singing competitions.

She was a finalist in the Burbank singing competition 2017.
